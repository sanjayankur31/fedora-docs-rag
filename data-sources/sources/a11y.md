# Vision

> Fedora Linux is the most comfortable desktop operating system to use
> for accessibility needs.

# Mission

> The Fedora Accessibility Working Group empowers Fedora to be the
> accessible distribution with a focus on four pillars:
>
> &#42; Create a designated place where people can raise issues about
> assistive technologies. &#42; Bring together stakeholders from
> different Fedora teams and different communities to solve
> accessibility issues. &#42; Educate accessibility developers about
> operating system requirements. &#42; Raise awareness about
> accessibility to the wider community.
