# ROS2 {#_ros2}

## Links and References {#_links_and_references_2}

- <https://gitlab.com/fedora/sigs/robotics/images/container-image-ros2>

- <https://gitlab.com/fedora/sigs/robotics/ros2-fedora-coreos>

- <https://docs.ros.org/en/rolling/index.html>

- <https://quay.io/organization/fedora-sig-robotics>

## Scope {#_scope_2}

We are looking into ways of making Fedora a friendlier environment for
ROS2 by:

- Building and maintaining non ROS2 packages within Fedora;

- Creating ROS2 container images for both development and production
  environments;

- Make ROS2 RPMs available in Fedora.
